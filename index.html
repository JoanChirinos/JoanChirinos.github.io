Software Engineering Experience

Joan started programming in the fall of 2016, when he took his first Intro to CS course. Since then, he has never stopped taking classes and doing his own side projects to help him learn as much as he can. He is proficient with most common data structures and algorithms, and can learn any new ones quickly.

He has interned with Two Sigma to learn Data Science, interned with Hexadoon studios to learn Game Development, and done various other projects, from a CLI-based Cards Against Humanity clone in C to a Satirical URL-lengthener, the latter of which won second place at Stuyhacks Fall 2018!
