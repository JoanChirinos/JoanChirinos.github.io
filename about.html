About

Joan Chirinos

Joan is studying Computer Science at Cornell University. He expects to graduate in May 2023 with a B.A. in Computer Science.

Joan is a Hispanic Scholarship Fund Scholar, and is the project manager of the RisleyArchives team at Cornell University.
